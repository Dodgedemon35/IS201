BMX Freestyle

BMX Freestyle is my Favorite sport, I have been riding BMX since the pandemic started. During the pandemic I could not find much to do and was going crazy trying being stuck in the house all the time. After browing through YouTube I found a video that would change my life. Some guy from Australia put out a video on Youtube in which he rode a BMX bike on all these crazy spots and he would do all of these crazy tricks.

After watching that video I was hooked, I got a job Lifeguarding and worked all summer to eventually buy a bike. From there I have ridden almost every day when the weather permits. I do this so I can progress and move forward in the sport.
